Greece

Greece

Zakynthos Island is famous for Navaio Beach — one of the most picturesque in Europe

About

One of the cradles of world civilization, sunny Greece attracts tourists with a variety of resorts, the purest turquoise waters of the Aegean Sea, developed infrastructure and high-quality service. Greek Mediterranean cuisine is one of the most healthy. The most important ancient monuments are concentrated in the country. Hotels for every taste and budget allow tourists to relax comfortably with any budget.

Main Goal

Every resort in Greece is unique with its pearls. The island of Crete is famous for the Palace of Knossos, a monument of the Minoan civilization, the cave of Zeus and the former Venetian city of Chania.

The best time to travel

From May to September. The island of Crete is best visited in August. Resorts in northern Greece — in June and July

Spain

Spain

Fuerteventura, the most draughty of the Canary Islands, is suitable for windsurfing

About

The archipelago in the Atlantic Ocean, located 1,700 km off the coast of Spain, is called the land of eternal summer. The weather is mild all year round — 26-28 ° C, but in summer the ocean warms up and is suitable for swimming. Here you can enjoy a relaxing holiday with children, practice surfing and diving, hike to volcanoes and contemplate the creations of nature.

Main Goal

Each of the islands of the archipelago is unique in its own way: it differs in beaches — pebble, sandy, volcanic, and natural attractions. The islands are connected by ferry service.

The best time to travel

If you are interested in eco-tourism in Tenerife, you should choose June: temperatures up to 24 ° C are favorable for hiking.

Norway

Norway

The basic route of cruise liners in Norway from Bergen is along fjords, including Sognefjord, Luce Fjord, Geiranger Fjord, Aurlands Fjord

About

Norway, or the Country of the Fjords, is a treasure trove of natural resources and unique Nordic landscapes, an example of respect for the environment, a country of unique culture and mythology. The climate here is harsh and unpredictable. There are almost constant piercing winds, cloudy weather on the territory of all 11 fylke (regions), and cold winters in the north. Summer is a short period when you can come to the country without suitcases with warm clothes, catch maximum sunny days, have picnics in nature, spend the night in a tent and ride a bike along mountain trails.

Main Goal

The main purpose of tourists in Norway is fjords. These picturesque narrow bays, cutting into the land along the entire coast for a distance of up to 200 km, are surrounded by bizarrely shaped rocks and green valleys.

The best time to travel

From June to August, the air warms up to 25 °C, there is a minimum of precipitation and most sunny days.
